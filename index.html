Achievements

Akshay Chibber Award
Won by Jai Kapoor for the year 2020.

The Akshay Chibber Memorial Award for All-Rounder of the Year is given to one or two students of class 12, who have displayed excellence in the field of academics as well as in sports,this year the winner was Jai Kapoor.
